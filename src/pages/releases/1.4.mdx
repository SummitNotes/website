**Transcription & Audio**

- Re-transcribe Segments - Right-click any conversation bubble to re-transcribe just that segment or from that point to the end. Perfect for fixing hallucinations or poor quality transcriptions without re-processing the entire recording.
- Click to Seek - Click conversation bubbles to jump directly to that position in the audio playback. Makes it easy to hear exactly what was said.
- Delete Conversation Bubbles - Remove individual transcription segments with a right-click context menu option.
- Auto-Stop Recording on Sleep - Recordings now automatically stop and finalize when your Mac goes to sleep, preventing data loss.

**AI & LLM Integration**

- Full Anthropic (Claude) Support - Complete integration with Anthropic's Claude API as an alternative to OpenAI, Google Gemini, or local models.
- Rename Placeholder Participants - AI-generated content now automatically replaces placeholder speaker names (like "Participant-abc123") with actual speaker names when you rename speakers.

**User Interface**

- Folder Icons in Sidebar - Visual folder icons now appear in the document sidebar for better organization.
- Fluent Audio Model Download Progress - Visual progress indicators when downloading new transcription models.
- Explicit Permissions Check - Clearer permission request flow with streamlined UX.
- In-App Update Checker - Automatic checking for new app versions with notifications.

**Organization & Management**

- Delete Tags and Speakers - Clean up unused tags and speakers directly from the UI.
- Speaker Merge Feature - Merge duplicate speaker profiles into a single identity.

**Bug Fixes**

- Fixed FluidAudio Confidence Data - Corrected confidence score handling for FluidAudio transcription model.
- Preserve User Titles - User-edited document titles are now preserved when re-running summarization.
- Rename Untitled Documents - Title field correctly maintained for documents without custom titles.
- Performance & Stability improvements
