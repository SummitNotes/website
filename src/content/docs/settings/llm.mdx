## Detailed Comparison

### Local AI (Built-in)

**What it is:**
AI models that run directly inside the Summit AI Notes app. No external software or servers needed.

**How it works:**
1. Download an AI model from within Summit settings (one-time)
2. Model runs on your Mac's chip when generating summaries
3. Everything happens inside the app

**Best for:**
- Users who want privacy without technical setup
- Consultants, lawyers, healthcare professionals
- Anyone with sensitive client meetings
- Budget-conscious users who record frequently

**Requirements:**
- Mac with M1 chip or newer
- 8-16GB RAM (more is better)
- 2-8GB disk space per model

**Setup time:** 1 minute + download time

---

### Cloud AI (Remote Services)

**What it is:**
Professional AI services from companies like OpenAI (ChatGPT), Anthropic (Claude), and Google (Gemini).

**How it works:**
1. Get an API key from your chosen provider
2. Enter it in Summit settings
3. Transcription text is sent to cloud for summarization
4. Summary comes back to your Mac

**Best for:**
- Users who want the best quality
- Teams who need fast processing
- Users with less powerful Macs
- Anyone willing to pay for convenience

**Available providers:**
- **Google Gemini** - Free tier available, good quality
- **OpenAI (ChatGPT)** - Industry standard, very reliable
- **Anthropic (Claude)** - Excellent for long meetings

**Setup time:** 2-5 minutes

---

### Self-Hosted AI (Your Own Server)

**What it is:**
Run your own AI server software (like LM Studio or Ollama) on your Mac, then connect Summit to it.

**How it works:**
1. Install LM Studio, Ollama, or similar software
2. Download and run AI models in that software
3. Configure Summit to connect to your local server
4. Summit sends transcription to your server (not the cloud)

**Best for:**
- Power users who want privacy + control
- Users who already run LM Studio/Ollama
- Technical users comfortable with server software
- Organizations with specific compliance requirements

**Popular options:**
- **LM Studio** - Easy GUI, good performance
- **Ollama** - Command line, very efficient
- **vLLM** - Advanced, maximum performance

**Setup time:** 15-30 minutes

### What Never Leaves Your Mac (All Options)

- ‚úÖ Original audio recordings
- ‚úÖ Meeting metadata (titles, dates, notes)
- ‚úÖ Your Summit AI Notes database

### What's Different

| Data Type | Local AI | Cloud AI | Self-Hosted |
|-----------|----------|----------|-------------|
| Audio | Stays local | Stays local | Stays local |
| Transcription | Stays local | Sent to cloud | Stays local |
| AI Processing | On your Mac | In the cloud | On your Mac/server |
| Summary | Stays local | Generated in cloud | Stays local |

## Can I Use Multiple Options?

**Yes!** You can switch between options anytime and even use different options for different meetings:

**Example Strategy:**
- **Local AI** for confidential client meetings
- **Cloud AI** for internal team meetings
- **Self-Hosted** for after you've set up LM Studio

**How to switch:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí AI Provider
2. Choose a different provider type
3. Your existing summaries stay unchanged

## Recommended Setups

### For Consultants/Professionals
**Start:** Cloud AI (Google Gemini free tier)
**Then:** Set up Local AI for sensitive meetings
**Later:** Add Self-hosted if you want more control

### For Privacy-Critical Work (Legal, Healthcare, Finance)
**Use:** Local AI exclusively
**Why:** Complete privacy, no external dependencies
**Trade-off:** Accept slightly slower processing

### For High Volume Users
**Use:** Cloud AI with budget models (GPT-3.5, Claude Haiku, Gemini Flash)
**Why:** Fast processing, reasonable cost
**Cost:** ~$10-15/month

### For Best Quality
**Use:** Cloud AI with premium models (GPT-4, Claude Opus)
**Why:** Best possible summaries
**Cost:** ~$20-30/month

### For Technical Users
**Use:** Self-hosted AI (LM Studio)
**Why:** Complete control, privacy, zero ongoing cost
**Trade-off:** Setup and maintenance time

### For Limited Budget + Privacy
**Use:** Local AI
**Why:** Free and private
**Trade-off:** Need decent Mac hardware
